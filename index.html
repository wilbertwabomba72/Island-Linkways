A calm puzzle about islands, bridges, and logic

Island Linkways turns classic bridge-building logic into a colorful beach puzzle. Each level asks you to read the numbers, plan the route, and link every island without crossing paths.

Numbered islands

Every island shows exactly how many bridges must touch it.

Clean bridge rules

Draw bridges only in straight horizontal or vertical lines. Bridges cannot cross or pass through another island.

Growing challenge

Progress through increasingly thoughtful layouts, from beginner boards to denser island networks.

Designed for iPhone and iPad

Large readable islands, calm tropical artwork, undo and hint controls, and board layouts that scale across phone and tablet screens.

How to play

1. Read the numbers

Each number tells you the total bridge connections required for that island.

2. Draw bridges

Swipe from one island to another in the same row or column to add or remove a bridge.

3. Complete one network

Solve the level when every island has the right count and all islands are connected together.
