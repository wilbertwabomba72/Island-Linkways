Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Island Linkways handles information when you use the game. If you have questions, contact us at 508923216@qq.com.

Information We Collect

Island Linkways stores gameplay progress, level completion, stars, and audio settings locally on your device. This information is used to provide core game features and is not used to identify you personally.

The game may show ads. Advertising and security SDKs may process limited device information, advertising identifiers where permitted, app usage events, approximate region, and diagnostics needed to deliver ads, prevent abuse, measure performance, and keep the app stable.

How We Use Information

We use information to save your progress, maintain game settings, improve stability, support advertising, and comply with platform requirements. We do not sell your personal information.

Third-Party Services

Island Linkways may use third-party advertising, analytics, or security providers. These providers process information according to their own privacy policies and only for app operation, ad delivery, measurement, fraud prevention, and diagnostics.

Children's Privacy

Island Linkways is a casual puzzle game. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Data Retention

Local game progress remains on your device until you delete the app or reset the relevant device data. Information handled by third-party services is retained according to those providers' policies.

Your Choices

You can limit ad tracking or reset your advertising identifier using your device settings. You can also delete the app to remove locally stored game progress from your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when the app changes or when legal requirements change. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.
